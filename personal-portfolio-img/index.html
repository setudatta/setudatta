About Me

Hi there! I’m Setu Datta, a passionate Business Analyst with over 4 years of experience in the software industry. I specialize in translating complex business requirements into actionable solutions, ensuring that both clients and teams are aligned for success.

Currently, I work at Think & Sync Ltd, where I collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather, analyze, and document business needs for software development projects. I focus on driving efficiency, reducing risks, and optimizing processes that lead to high-quality software products.

My diverse skill set extends beyond business analysis, and includes UI & UX Design, Quality Control (QC), Data Collection, Customer Interfacing, Customer Support, Project Management, and Team Leadership. I am experienced in creating User Requirements Specifications (URS), facilitating User Training, and leading Project Implementations to ensure that projects are delivered on time and meet all client expectations.

I also have hands-on experience in Agile methodologies, as well as proficiency with JIRA, SQL, TRELLO, ODOO PROJECT MANAGEMENT etc. I believe that clear communication, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of both the technical and business sides of a project are key to delivering exceptional results.

When I’m not optimizing workflows or conducting data analysis, you can find me exploring new technologies, attending industry webinars, or working on my latest passion project.

I’m always open to connecting with others in the tech and business fields to share ideas and collaborate. Feel free to reach out if you’d like to chat!
